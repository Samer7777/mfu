Introduction:

The Central Bank of Sudan, in the light of the five-year plan to implement the vision, established in March 2007 the Microfinance Unit as a semi-autonomous entity administratively and financially, affiliated with the financial institutions and systems sector. Effective institutions and activities aimed at eliminating poverty among sectors of society. Promoting productive activity to achieve balanced economic development throughout the country, in accordance with the plan of the peace agreement
